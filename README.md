# Professional Resume Template

A clean, modern resume template designed to help you stand out to recruiters and showcase your experience effectively.

## Key Features

* **Interactive Design:** Explore the resume easily using built-in filters that highlight your specific skills and experience as you click.
* **Professional Printing:** The template automatically cleans itself up for printing, ensuring your exported PDF or physical copy looks sharp, professional, and is completely free of web-only elements.
* **Ready to Use:** Built to be plug-and-play, you don't need any special software or technical setup to get started; just open the file and begin editing.
* **Organized Content:** Features a modern layout with optional details, allowing you to hide or show extra information to keep your resume concise and easy to read.
* **Visual Consistency:** Designed with elegant, professional typography and a balanced, high-quality aesthetic that helps your profile stand out to recruiters.
